    About Me
    Hi! My name is Etienne and I am currently enrolled in a Coding Bootcamp for Full-Stack Development at Columbia University/Trilogy. I am interested in becoming a web developer to work on web design, development and interactive 3D content.
    Previously, I have taken courses in Javascript, SQL and Java and I have a Certificate in 3D Design and Fabrication (3D Printing) from NYU and some familiarity with 3D softwares (Cinema 4D and Zbrush).
